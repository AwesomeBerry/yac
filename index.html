Our campaign

Our core values is centered on I.M.P.A.C.T

These values shape every action, decision, and message we share.
These values — Integrity, Mobilization, Participation, Accountability, Creativity, and Transformation — capture the principles that guide our work and define what YAC stands for. Together, they provide a unified framework for empowering young people to lead meaningful anti‑corruption efforts.


I - Integrity

More Than a Message. It’s our DNA. We don’t just preach anti-corruption; we live it. We’re fostering a space where bold honesty shapes how we lead, collaborate, and grow.
 
 

Read More

M - Mobilization

Stronger Together. We don't just advocate; we activate. Recognizing that collective action is our greatest tool, we offer a launchpad for youth to collaborate and lead the charge for an honest future.
 

Read More

P - Participation

Driven by You. We believe the best solutions come from those most affected. We centre youth in every decision, providing the tools, from local chapters to global digital advocacy, to help you build and lead your own fight against corruption.

Read More

A - Accountability

Built on Trust. We lead with openness because we know that secrecy is where corruption begins. Our youth-driven mission is defined by responsibility—to each other and to our cause—ensuring that every action we take is clear, honest, and impactful.

Read More

C - Creativity

Bold Minds, Big Change. We engage young people through the lens of innovation and creativity. From digital art to viral advocacy, we provide the space for your creativity to flourish, proving that imagination is our most powerful weapon against corruption.

Read More

T - Transformation

Change That Lasts. We’re here to shift the status quo. By focusing on deep-rooted systemic change and measurable impact, we are building a foundation of honesty that will stand the test of time.
 

Read More
